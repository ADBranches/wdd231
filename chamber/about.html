About Kambale Chamber of Commerce

Our Mission

To advance the economic prosperity of our members and the Kambale community by providing leadership, advocacy, and resources that foster business growth and development.

We are committed to creating a vibrant business environment where entrepreneurship can thrive, businesses can connect, and our community can prosper together.

Our History

1995

Foundation

The Kambale Chamber of Commerce was established by local business leaders to support economic development in the region.

2005

Expansion

Membership grew to over 200 businesses, and we launched our first community development initiative.

2015

Digital Transformation

Launched our online directory and digital resources to better serve members in the digital age.

2023

Present Day

Now serving 500+ members with comprehensive business support services and community programs.
